Dear People

I've always wondered where thine and own anxiety stem from.
Numerous days and nights where told thee there is a solely shining star, thou continued to fade away.

Now that I have entered a greater world, I shall see.
I see how thou hath been afraid of the brightly shining stars in the night sky on a starry night that would seemingly dim thy light.

Hence it mustn't be the other stars that dim thou, a star fades away when it realizes itself to be old and meek.

Coil between us stemmed from wronged fancies and excuses.
Now that I shall see, there art to be stars that shine through and art not to dim

- From a letter by my dearest friend Stella.
